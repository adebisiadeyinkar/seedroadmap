Crops

We presently work with Coffee, Cacao Beans and Tea Plant.

Coffee

Coffee bean is a seed of coffee plant and the source for coffee. It is the pit inside the red or purple fruit often reffered to as cherry. Just like ordinary cherries, the coffee fruit is also a so-called stone fruit.

Cacao Beans

Cacao bean or simply cocoa is the dried and fully fermented seed of Theobroma cacao, from which cocoa solid powder and cocoa butter can be extracted.

Tea Plant

The tea plant is an evergreen shrub that provides us with black, white, yellow, and green tea as well as oolong and pu-erh tea. It is the leaves and leaf buds that are commonly used to produce the teas we enjoy. ... The tea plant can live anywhere between 30 and 50 years.
